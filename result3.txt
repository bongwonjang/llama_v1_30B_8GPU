How can I go to New York from Seoul?
You can choose between different transportation options. You can travel by plane, train or bus.
The shortest distance is of about 10 000 kilometers. The plane is the fastest way to travel, but it is also the most expensive.
You can also travel by train and the route is about 15 000 kilometers. It is quite long and it takes a lot of time. The price is not so high and depends on the type of train.
